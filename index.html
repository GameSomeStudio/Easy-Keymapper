ℹ️ About KeyMapper

🎮 Easy KeyMapper

Drag & drop keys to remap your keyboard - Optimized for gaming

⚠️ Important: This tool is designed for gaming only. Do not use while typing text — both original and remapped keys will be sent simultaneously in text fields.
No registry changes are made. All remapping is temporary and works only while KeyMapper is running. Keyboard resets to default automatically when you exit the application.
⌨️ Ctrl+Shift+K: Toggle remapping ON/OFF instantly — ON for gaming, OFF for typing. Status appears on screen and system tray.
